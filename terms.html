Official Terms & Conditions

Service rules and guidelines laid out by Pragati Car Carriers.

1. Service Undertaking

Pragati Car Carriers offers customized car shipping, motorcycle moving, and associated vehicle storage structures for both single individuals and commercial corporate entities nationwide within India.

2. Vehicle State Verification

A standard car/bike survey form must be filled out detailing existing dents and physical features prior to shipping loading. All bookings accept the clause that internal engine mechanics or pre-existing minor surface defects are not covered by logistical liability.

3. Personal Assets Restrictions

No expensive gadgets, loose vehicle accessories, or private properties should be left in cars or bikes during transport logistics. Our group holds no liability for unchecked assets inside the cargo storage containers.

4. Advance Transactions

Bookings are confirmed upon receiving the specified percentage tokens. Full final payment balances must be settled digitally or via designated cash channels prior to physical handovers at the final destination hub.
