Terms & Conditions

Please read our service terms carefully before booking your vehicle transportation.

1. Vehicle Condition & Inspection

Before loading, a detailed condition report/inspection sheet of the car or bike will be filled by our team. Existing dents, scratches, and vehicle fuel levels must be correctly documented and signed by both parties.

2. Personal Belongings

Customers are strictly advised not to leave any personal belongings, valuables, cash, or expensive electronics inside the vehicle during transit. Pragati Car Carriers will not be responsible for any loss of personal items.

3. Insurance Policy

All vehicles transit under valid transit insurance. In case of any unforeseen structural damage during shipping, the claim will be processed directly as per the standard insurance provider norms. Minor engine technical issues or tyre punctures are not covered.

4. Delivery Timeline

While we always ensure on-time delivery, timelines are estimated. Deliveries may get delayed due to unexpected traffic conditions, government border checks, bad weather, or festive holidays.

5. Payment Terms

A specific advance percentage must be paid at the time of vehicle pickup, and the remaining balance amount must be fully cleared before or at the time of delivery.
